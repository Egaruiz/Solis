Lawn Maitenace In Ann Arbor:

Solis Lawn Care, based in Ann Arbor, offers comprehensive lawn maintenance services to keep your yard healthy, vibrant, and aesthetically pleasing throughout the year. Whether you're a homeowner or a business, their team of skilled professionals provides a wide range of lawn care services designed to enhance the beauty and health of your lawn.

>Services Offered :

Mowing and Edging:

Regular mowing is essential for maintaining a neat and tidy lawn. Solis Lawn Care offers professional mowing services, ensuring that your grass is kept at the optimal height. They also provide edging along sidewalks, driveways, and flower beds to give your lawn a crisp, clean look.

Fertilization

and Weed Control: The company applies specialized fertilizers to ensure your lawn has the right nutrients for healthy growth. They also offer effective weed control treatments, preventing the growth of unwanted plants and helping to maintain a lush, green lawn.

Aeration:

Lawn aeration is crucial for improving soil health and allowing air, water, and nutrients to penetrate deep into the soil. Solis Lawn Care performs aeration to relieve compacted soil and enhance grass root growth.

Seeding:

Over time, grass may thin out or get damaged. Solis Lawn Care provides overseeding services to fill in bare spots and promote a fuller, healthier lawn. They choose the right seed mix for Ann Arbor's climate to ensure successful germination.

Mulching

and Trimming: Solis Lawn Care offers mulching services to help retain moisture, regulate soil temperature, and reduce weed growth. Trimming of shrubs, hedges, and trees is also available to keep your landscaping well-maintained.

Seasonal Clean-up:

Solis Lawn Care provides seasonal clean-up services, including leaf removal in the fall and debris clearing in the spring, so your lawn is ready for the changing seasons.

Lawn Disease and Pest Management:

They can identify and treat common lawn diseases and pests that can damage your grass, ensuring your lawn stays healthy year-round.
