Snow Removal Ann Arbor, Solis Lawn Care llc

When winter hits Michigan, snow and ice can quickly become a nuisance, making driveways, walkways, and parking lots hazardous. At Solis Lawn Care, we’re here to help Ypsilanti, and surrounding areas stay safe and accessible throughout the season. We specialize in reliable, professional snow removal services, ensuring your property is clear of snow and ice, so you can navigate your home or business with ease.

Why Choose Solis Lawn Care for Snow Removal?

Local Expertise

We know the unique weather patterns in the Ann Arbor areas. From heavy snowfalls to unexpected icy conditions, our team is prepared for whatever winter throws our way. With years of experience serving the community, we understand the importance of timely, thorough snow removal to keep your property safe.

Professional Equipment

At Solis Lawn Care, we use state-of-the-art snow removal equipment to ensure that your driveway, sidewalk, or commercial property is cleared quickly and effectively. Whether it's a light dusting or a heavy snowstorm, we have the tools to handle it, including plows, shovels, salt spreaders, and snow blowers.

4/7 Availability

Snow doesn’t follow a schedule, and neither do we. Our snow removal services are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, so no matter when the snow falls, we’re ready to respond. Whether you need pre-storm treatments or post-storm clean-up, we’re here to help.

Residential and Commercial Services

From small residential driveways to large commercial parking lots, we handle snow removal jobs of all sizes. Whether you're a homeowner who needs a reliable service to keep your driveway clear or a business owner who needs to ensure your parking lot is safe for customers and employees, Solis Lawn Care has you covered.

Safe, Efficient, and Eco-Friendly

Our snow removal methods are safe for your property and the environment. We use eco-friendly ice melt products when possible to avoid damaging your landscaping or surrounding surfaces. Our team is trained to handle snow removal efficiently and effectively, ensuring minimal disruption to your day.

Affordable Pricing

We believe that professional snow removal should be accessible and affordable. Our competitive pricing is designed to fit within your budget without compromising on the quality of service. We offer flexible pricing options for both residential and commercial customers, including per-storm rates and seasonal contracts.

Driveway Snow Plowing:

Sidewalk Snow Shoveling & Salting:

Parking Lot Snow Removal:

24/7 Emergency Snow Removal:
