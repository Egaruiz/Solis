Landscaping Services in Ann Arbor by Solis Lawn Care

Solis Lawn Care is a trusted landscaping service provider based in Ann Arbor, Michigan, known for offering professional, reliable, and comprehensive lawn care solutions. Specializing in enhancing the beauty and health of residential and commercial landscapes, Solis Lawn Care provides a wide range of services designed to meet the unique needs of each client.

Services Offered by Solis Lawn Care

Lawn Maintenance

Regular lawn maintenance is essential for a lush, green, and healthy lawn. Solis Lawn Care offers routine lawn mowing, edging, trimming, and weed control. Their team ensures that every lawn is well-kept, properly trimmed, and free from unsightly weeds.

Landscape Design and Installation

Whether you're starting from scratch or enhancing an existing outdoor space, Solis Lawn Care provides expert landscape design services. They work closely with clients to create beautiful, functional designs that align with personal preferences and enhance the overall curb appeal of properties. Their design services may include planting trees, shrubs, flowers, and installing hardscapes like patios, pathways, and retaining walls.

Fertilization and Soil Care

A healthy lawn requires proper nutrition, and Solis Lawn Care offers expert fertilization services to promote growth and prevent soil depletion. They provide tailored fertilization schedules that suit the specific needs of different grass types, ensuring your lawn stays vibrant year-round.

Seasonal Cleanup

Ann Arbor experiences all four seasons, and Solis Lawn Care offers seasonal cleanup services, including fall leaf removal and spring cleanups. These services keep your lawn and garden areas clear of debris and ready for the changing seasons.

Irrigation System Installation and Maintenance

To keep your lawn and plants hydrated, Solis Lawn Care installs and maintains irrigation systems, including sprinklers and drip systems. These systems are designed to ensure efficient water usage, saving time and resources while maintaining a healthy landscape.

Tree and Shrub Care

Proper tree and shrub maintenance is crucial for their longevity. Solis Lawn Care offers pruning, trimming, and health assessments to ensure your trees and shrubs thrive and contribute to your landscape's overall beauty.

Mulching and Bed Care

Mulch is an important part of maintaining healthy garden beds. Solis Lawn Care provides mulching services that protect plant roots, prevent weeds, and retain moisture. They also offer bed care services, including edging, planting, and maintenance.

Hardscaping

Solis Lawn Care specializes in designing and installing hardscaping elements such as patios, fire pits, retaining walls, walkways, and decorative stone features. Their hardscaping services enhance the functionality and aesthetics of outdoor spaces, creating beautiful and durable outdoor living areas.

Why Choose Solis Lawn Care in Ann Arbor?

Expertise and Experience: With years of experience in the landscaping industry, Solis Lawn Care's team has the knowledge and skills to handle any landscaping project, big or small.

Customer-Focused Approach: Solis Lawn Care is committed to providing personalized service. They work closely with clients to understand their preferences and create customized solutions.

Reliable Service: Known for their professionalism and attention to detail, Solis Lawn Care ensures that every project is completed on time and to the highest standards.

Eco-Friendly Practices: Solis Lawn Care prioritizes sustainability by using eco-friendly products and practices whenever possible, such as organic fertilizers and efficient irrigation systems.

Whether you're looking to transform your lawn, add new landscaping features, or simply maintain a healthy outdoor environment, Solis Lawn Care in Ann Arbor provides the expertise and services to help you achieve your landscaping goals.
